Tips

List of useful hacks regarding plants

  1. Clear pots for better view of the root health
  2. Use lemon to remove water stain on the leaf
  3. Cat litter can be applied instead of perlite
  4. Cinnamons for fungal infection
  5. For fertiliser boil tea + oates then let it cool, sift the bits and water your plant with it
  6. Use potato water (without soil) which contain potassium
  7. Put something at the top soil like moss to keep moisture
  8. Container from milk user to water plants, the big size allow contain more water
  9. Water plant before the cutting the propagation
  10. Place plants close together to create higher humidity
  11. User any clear lid for propagation to make better humidity increasing success of it
  12. Introduce support pole at early stage of plant growth
  13. Start from small size plant, this will make the plant more adaptable for your home environment
  14. Regularly pruning as this will stimulate new growth
  15. Only change to bigger pot if the root start peaking from the bottom of the pot
  16. Ricotta plant pot evaporate water faster, to avoid use apply clear sealer inside
  17. Individually decide how often to water the plant
  18. Local charity shops are best place to get your plant pot
  19. Use your propagation as a gift for others
  20. Aloe vera can be user as rooting hormone for propagation
